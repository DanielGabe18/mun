Model United Nations helps students learn more about the principles of the United Nations and how it works. Many of today's leaders in law, government, business and the arts (including at the UN itself) participated in Model PBB while still students.

On this occasion, the 2024 Victory Plus Model United Nations (SVPMUN) School is the only Model PBB event for Middle School-Senior High School classes in Bekasi.

SVPMUN 2024 combines various events ranging from the Model UN Conference, training, to socialization events between participants. The UN conference model is designed to hone public speaking and explore research and critical thinking skills. Approximately 150 middle and high school participants from various schools throughout Indonesia are targeted to take part in this event. SVPMUN trains them to compete and be responsible for the country delegations they choose during the conference. Then each delegation met individually to discuss various topics related to the general theme determined by each UN division. In dealing with each topic, each delegation strives to achieve its country's goals. In the end, all delegations and conference committees worked together to create a series of resolutions, to address existing problems, and submitted them to the Model UN committee. During the conference, Model United Nations uses 100% English so this event is also an opportunity to actualize English language skills.


The MUN training workshop was open to the public for two days, held exclusively by UI MUN and SVPMUN. Aiming to train public speaking, research and critical thinking skills, this training will prepare participants for a simulation of the upcoming UN conference. We are targeting approximately 150 participants to take part in this training. Implementation Date: Saturday, March 2 2024 Sunday, March 3 2024.


The main event of SVPMUN 2024 is a simulation or model of the UN conference which will be held for 2 days. This PBB model is paid at the price attached in the "Registration Price List" section. Each country's delegation meets individually to discuss various topics related to the general theme determined by each UN division. We are targeting approximately 150 participants to take part in this training. Implementation Date: Saturday, April 20 2024 Sunday, April 21 2024.
